SpawningMethod

Overview

Set parameters for specifying the position where particles are generated first. In addition to the influence of parent particle position on child particles, the value set here affects. That is, particles are generated around the position moved and rotated by the value specified by this spawning method, with the position of the parent particle as the origin.

Parameter

Set angle on spawn

Specify whether the parameter of the spawning method affects not only the position but also the angle. For example, if you specify "sphere", the Y direction of the generated particles will be the normal direction of the sphere.

Checked Unchecked

Method of Spawning

Specify how to set the generation position.

Point

Treats the point at the specified position as the generation position.

Circle

Place the particles in a circle. At this time, specify the radius and rotation angle. When checking "influence on generation angle" The upward direction of the particle is the circumference direction of the circle.

Sphere

Place the particles in a spherical shape. At this time, specify the radius and rotation angle of X and Y axes. When checking "influence on generation angle" The Y direction of the particle is the circumference direction of the circle.

Model

Place particles along the shape of the model for Effekseer. When checking "influence on generation angle", the Z direction of the particle is the normal direction of the model.

Spawn Mode
Random

Place particles randomly from the surface of the model.

Vertex

Place particles sequentially from the top of the model.

Random Vertex

We randomly place particles from the top of the model.

Surface

Place the particles in order from the center of the model's face.

Random Surface

Place particles randomly from the center of the face of the model.
