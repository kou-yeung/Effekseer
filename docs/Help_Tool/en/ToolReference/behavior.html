Behavior

Overview

Sets how to display effects on the editor. Representations close to the game can be reproduced, such as multiple display.

Parameter

Pos

Specify the position of the particle at the root of the first frame's effect.

Speed

Specify the movement speed of the particle at the root of the effect.

Angle

Specify the angle of the particle at the root of the first frame's effect.

Angular Speed

Specify the rotation speed of the particle at the root of the effect.

Scale

Specify the enlargement of the particle at the root of the first frame's effect.

Expansion Rate

Specify the enlargement speed of the particle at the root of the effect.

Life

Specify the time (frame unit) to delete particles of the route while displaying the effect.

X Count

Specify the number when displaying effects side by side in X direction.

Y Count

Specify the number when displaying effects side by side in Y direction.

Z Count

Specify the number when displaying effects side by side in Z direction.

Separation

Specify the distance between effects when displaying multiple effects.

Point of Attraction

Specify the coordinates of the target when the effect gathers on the target.
